## Project Description
In this scenario, I acted as a security analyst for "Botium Toys", a fictional toy company expanding into the U.S. and E.U. markets. The company needed an internal security audit to identify vulnerabilities, assess risks, and ensure compliance with relevant standards.

## Objective
To assess the current security posture, identify risks to critical assets, and recommend controls to mitigate them, ensuring compliance with PCI DSS and GDPR.

## Key Findings & Recommendations
Based on the audit of internal controls, I prioritized the following actions:
1.  **Implement Encryption (Critical):** Credit card data is currently unencrypted. Implementing encryption is mandatory for PCI DSS compliance and to protect customer trust.
2.  **Enforce Least Privilege:** Currently, all employees have access to sensitive data. Access should be restricted (RBAC) so employees only see what is necessary for their specific roles.
3.  **Deploy Backups & Disaster Recovery:** The lack of backups poses a catastrophic risk to business continuity. Automated backups must be established immediately.
